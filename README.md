🛡️ **Insurance Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)**

This project presents a comprehensive Insurance Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze insurance claims, policy performance, customer demographics, and operational KPIs. The dashboard provides a centralized analytical view of claim activity, approval trends, premium distribution, and loss ratios, enabling insurance providers to make informed, data-driven business decisions.
The solution is designed with a focus on financial performance monitoring, customer segmentation, and risk analysis, helping stakeholders identify high-risk policies, optimize claim approvals, and improve operational efficiency.

🔍 **Overview**

The dashboard delivers high-level business insights through key KPIs.The interface follows a structured, executive-level layout with dark-theme visualization styling, KPI cards, interactive filters, and drill-down analytics for enhanced usability and storytelling.

📊 **Key Insights & Analysis**

- Claims & Premium Analysis
Compared Total Claim Amount vs Total Premium across multiple policy IDs to evaluate policy profitability and financial exposure.
Identified high-claim policies which contribute significantly to claim payouts and higher loss ratios.
- Policy Type Performance
Analyzed claim volume and approval rates across Health, Auto, and Life insurance categories.
Health insurance policies recorded the highest claim count and strongest approval trends, indicating higher customer utilization.
- Regional Analysis
Evaluated claim distribution across North, South, and West regions.
West region generated the highest claim amount, highlighting geographical concentration of insurance risk.
- Customer Demographics
Performed gender-based and age-group analysis to understand customer claim behavior.
Implemented Loss Ratio Analysis to assess claim-to-premium relationships and identify financially risky policies.
Tracked average claim amount per policy to support underwriting and premium optimization strategies.
- Operational Performance
Measured approval rates and transaction trends to evaluate operational efficiency and claim processing performance.

🛠️ **Technical Implementation**

Built a robust data model integrating claims, policy, customer, and premium datasets
-Created optimized DAX measures for:
    - Total Claims
    - Loss Ratio
    - Approval Rate
    - Average Claim Amount
    - Premium Analysis
- Designed interactive visuals including:
    - KPI Cards
    - Bar Charts
    - Clustered Charts
    - Pie Charts
    - Matrix Tables
- Added interactive slicers for:
    - Policy Type
    - Claim Status
    - Week Start Date
Applied consistent UI/UX principles with a professional dark-theme dashboard design and visual hierarchy.

This dashboard helps insurance organizations to:

- Identify high-risk policies and regions
- Improve claim approval efficiency
- Analyze customer demographics and claim behavior
- Optimize premium and underwriting strategies
Support operational and financial decision-making through actionable insights
